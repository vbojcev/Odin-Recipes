Soup has been a household staple worldwide for thousands of years!

Ingredients:

  • Carrots and Parseley
  • Water
  • Chicken broth
  • Noodles

Steps:

  1. Chop up carrots and Parseley
  2. Boil water
  3. Add chicken broth
  4. Add noodles
  5. Boil water until noodles are softened
  6. Take off heat, let cool momentarily, and serve
